Sleep Tracker Help Sleep Tracker Help How to log overnight sleep Overnight sleep can be logged for any day

Input your dates into the scroll menus and click done when you are finished

Once both times have been chosen click the Calculate Sleep Hours button to see how many hours were slept

If you would like to log the number click on the submit sleep hours button!

Go to page! How to log sleepiness during the day! There are 7 options of sleepiness based on the stanford sleepiness scale

To select the option that you would like to log simply push the gray box to the right of the one that alligns with your current sleepiness

Click submit to log this data for your current time

Sleepiness can be logged many times throughout the day as needed

Go to page! How to view logged data Logged data is displayed in the data page

The data will display whether it was overnight logged data or sleepiness data

Data that you inputted incorrectly or no longer need can be deleted by sliding the data to the left and clicking delete
